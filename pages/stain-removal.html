Service Overview

Stain Removal

Some marks come out with a standard clean and some need more focused work. {{ site.business_name }} offers stain removal treatments for carpets and upholstery where a normal clean is not enough and a more targeted approach is needed. This service is ideal for common food spills, tracked in marks, general spot treatment and selected problem areas that need extra attention.

No honest cleaner can promise every stain will disappear completely, because some staining permanently alters the fibre or backing. What can be offered is careful assessment, the right treatment sequence and an honest view of the likely outcome before work begins.

Stain removal treatments can be booked as part of a wider carpet or upholstery clean, or as a targeted add on for the rooms and furniture that need the most attention. It is often the difference between a room looking merely cleaner and looking properly improved.
